Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have an intimate relationship with the produce they consume. That's why we're introducing a system that intelligently grows your favorite fruits and vegetables with hydroponic technology right in your urban apartment complex, providing an easy and affordable way for you to access fresh produce at home while minimizing your waste and carbon footprint.
