Hello there!

My name is Isaiah Yoon. I am a 21 year-old senior (to-be!) undergraduate at UC Berkeley born and raised in Koreatown, Los Angeles. I attended Brentwood Science Magnet for elementary school and the Los Angeles Center for Enriched Studies (LACES) for middle and high school.

Outside of my computer science interests, I enjoy playing and watching basketball, powerlifting, hiking scenic locations, and serving The Presence, a Christian community at UC Berkeley.
