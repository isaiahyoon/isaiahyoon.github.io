Welcome to my page!

My name is Isaiah Yoon. and I am a 19 year-old sophomore undergraduate at UC Berkeley. I was born and raised in Koreatown, located in the middle of Los Angeles. For elementary school, I went to Brentwood Science Magnet and for middle/high school, I attended the Los Angeles Center for Enriched Studies (better known as LACES). At UC Berkeley, I am studying computer science and cognitive science. With my degree I hope to either work as a software engineer or research artificial intelligence, specifically in natural language processing.

Some of my recreational interests include watching NBA basketball, lifting weights, and browsing Reddit.

If you happen to see me walking by on campus, say what's up! I'd be glad to have a conversation with you, as long as I'm not rushing to my morning classes. You can usually tell by the bed hair.
