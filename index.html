Hello there!

My name is Isaiah Yoon, a 20 year-old junior undergraduate at UC Berkeley. Born and raised in Koreatown, Los Angeles, I attended Brentwood Science Magnet for elementary school and the Los Angeles Center for Enriched Studies (LACES) for middle and high school.


At UC Berkeley, I am majoring in computer science and cognitive science. With my degree I hope to go into high school education and teach computer science and coach strength training.


Outside of my computer science interests, I enjoy playing and watching basketball, powerlifting, hiking scenic locations, and serving The Presence, a Christian fellowship at UC Berkeley.
