The Pomodoro technique is a time management method.


This particular clock allots 25 minutes for the work session, 5 minutes for the breaks, and 15 minutes for every fourth break.


Break timers start automatically.

Work timers only begin after you press ENTER.

A ding will go off when there's one minute left in the work session.


To begin, press the ENTER key or click here.

To exit at any time, press the ESC key or refresh the page.
